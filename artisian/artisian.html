Fleet Foxes

Biography

Fleet Foxes are an American band formed in Seattle, Washington.They are an indie folk band known for their use of refined lyrics and vocal harmonies. They became increasingly popular by 2007, when their MySpace page got over a quarter of a million song plays within 2 months. After that, they signed with the record label Sub Pop in 2008. For a while, they were more popular in Europe than the US.

Fleet Foxes has 5 members; . Pecknold and Skjelset were the 2 that originally bonded and started making music together; influenced by their parents' music tastes. They were also inspired by Bob Dylan and Brian Wilson, which made them realize the importance of practicing music from a young age. The original name of the band was going to be "The Pineapples" but was changed to "Fleet Foxes" because it clashed with another local band name.

The band has released 2 albums. One titles "Fleet Foxes" made in 2008 and the other "Helplessness Blues" made in 2011. They have also released several singles and music videos. In 2013 there was speculation of a possible third album to be released, but no further moves have been made since then.
